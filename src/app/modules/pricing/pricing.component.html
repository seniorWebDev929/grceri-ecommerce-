Commonly Asked Questions

  • If I switch from yearly to monthly, do I lose any data?

    No, you do not lose any data, we restrict what data you can access based on your plan. Only if you go down a tier you will lose data based on cap restrictions.

  • Do you offer refunds if I want to cancel?

    No, all charges are final. This prevent users from abusing the service by spamming accounts.

  • What nutritional information do you provide?

    We provide product nutrition to help your decisions when shopping. Set limits on your diet by viewing personalized results and recommendations to stay on track.

  • Do you provide a free trial with all paid plans?

    All paid plans have a 15-day trial with no obligation, risk free and automated. No humans required.

  • If I don't like the service am I charged a cancellation fee?

    No, there is no cancellation fee. Everything is automated, and accessible to put on hold or cancel on your dashboard.

  • What does your product comparison do?

    This allows you to compare multiple products on every detail from pricing, to nutrition data, rating, etc. This helps your decision making when looking for differences between similiar food types.

  • How accurate is your product data?

    All our data is the most accurate information you can find online today, we search for all online vendors that sell products you're interested in. All in one place, convienence at your fingertips.

  • Can I customize the types of notifications I get?

    Yes, in your dashboard portal you will be able to choose what types of notifications and the frequency. If you decide on selecting a few or deciding not to have any you do that too.
